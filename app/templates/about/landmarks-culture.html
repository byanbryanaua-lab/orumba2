Landmarks & Culture

Where tradition meets modern progress

Agulu Lake

Mystical lake with crocodile legends and fishing traditions. Major cultural landmark drawing visitors from across Anambra.

Igboukwu Bronzes

9th century bronze artifacts proving sophisticated Igbo civilization predating Europe. UNESCO heritage site candidate.

New Yam Festival

Annual Iri Ji celebration with masquerades, wrestling, traditional music. Thanksgiving for bountiful harvest.

Nri Sacred Sites

Ancient spiritual capital of Igbo people. Birthplace of Eze Nri - ritual king whose influence shaped Igbo society.

Agricultural Heritage

Yam capital of Anambra. Traditional farming techniques, communal labor systems, market days that define community life.

Traditional Titles

Ozo, Ichie, Nze systems. Community leaders who preserve customs, mediate disputes, organize festivals.
